How to trigger events

Click on a learner below to view their details and trigger Fourbyfour events. Each event triggers an automated workflow:

  • course.enrolled → Welcome & onboarding workflow
  • course.abandoned → Course re-engagement workflow
  • streak.broken → Streak recovery workflow
  • certificate.earned → Celebration & upsell workflow
